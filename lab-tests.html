المنهج · The rationale

We read the root, not only the surface.

At BATO, true beauty starts from within and shows on the outside. Hair that thins and skin that loses its calm often trace back to something internal: a vitamin running low, a hormone out of balance, an iron level that quietly dropped. Treating the surface alone leaves that cause untouched.

That is why diagnostics sit beside our hair and skin care. Your results are read and explained, and a plan follows from there. We make no promises about results. We make a careful, honest start.
