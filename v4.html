العناية بالشعر · Hair

Hairالشعر

For thinning or tired hair, we look at what's happening underneath, including your bloodwork, then treat the scalp with mesotherapy, plasma (PRP) and targeted care. Medically supervised, and honest about what care can do.

العناية بالبشرة · Skin

Skinالبشرة

We look at your skin and your goals first, then plan peels, laser and skincare to suit it. Clarity, gently restored.

النحت · Fillers & Botox

The measured handاللمسة المدروسة

الجمال يبدأ بالتحاليل

Beauty, tested first.

Before any treatment, we can read your bloodwork, hormones, vitamins, iron and thyroid, the quiet things that shape your hair and skin. Samples go to an accredited laboratory, and every plan is approved by our supervising physician.
