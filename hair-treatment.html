القراءة · The reading

It starts with a diagnosis, not a sale.

Before anything is recommended, a specialist reads your scalp under close light and a trichoscope: the density of the hair, the health of each follicle, the condition of the skin beneath. Thinning has many causes, and they do not all answer to the same care.

We hold to one rule. We never sell a treatment before we have read the scalp. The reading shapes the plan, and the plan is honest about what care can and cannot do.

Hair also begins within. Iron, ferritin, vitamin D, thyroid and hormones leave their mark on the scalp, so a reading on the surface often pairs with a look within. We read the root causes, on the skin and under it.
